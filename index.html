About Us

Canberra-based. Government-trusted. Results-driven.

Who we are

Mayi Pty Ltd is a Canberra-based technology company with deep roots in Australia's public sector. We bring decades of combined technical expertise to every engagement — from complex ICT integrations to cutting-edge data science platforms — with the personal attention that only a specialist firm can provide.

Located in the heart of the national capital, we understand the unique challenges, compliance requirements, and procurement frameworks of government-grade technology delivery.

What makes us different

  • Government Expertise. We know APS procurement, security frameworks (ISM, Essential Eight), and policy requirements — no learning curve, no surprises.
  • Senior-Led Delivery. Your project is led by senior engineers and architects, not offshored to junior teams.
  • Transparent Pricing. Clear milestones and fixed-scope engagements — no surprise bills at invoice time.
  • Security-First Design. Every solution is designed with ACSC's Essential Eight and the ISM in mind from day one.

Cyber Security

Cyber Threat Intelligence Platform

Federal Government Agency · 2023

Challenge

The agency lacked consolidated visibility of threats across its distributed ICT estate, relying on manual processes that couldn't scale with the growing threat landscape.

Solution

Mayi designed and built a centralised threat intelligence platform integrating feeds from multiple sources, with automated alerting aligned to the ACSC Essential Eight framework.

Outcome

60% reduction in mean time to detect incidents. Platform now operational across three agencies.

System Integration

Legacy System Modernisation

National Infrastructure Provider · 2022

Challenge

Critical business processes were locked in a 15-year-old monolithic system with no APIs, preventing integration with modern tooling and creating unacceptable operational risk.

Solution

Mayi delivered a phased strangler-fig migration, wrapping the legacy core with modern APIs and progressively replacing modules with zero planned downtime.

Outcome

Full integration achieved within 9 months. System reliability improved from 97.2% to 99.8% uptime.
